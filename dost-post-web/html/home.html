Some Thoughts

Rohit Bansal

          Rohit has more than 20 years of experience in leading engineering teams with a focus on guiding organisational behaviour. Prior to co-founding Sahaj, Rohit played multiple leadership roles with ThoughtWorks for more than 15 years. Rohit established the operations of ThoughtWorks India in 2001 and was one of the pioneers in evolving the practices for distributed agile development. His interest in social development led him to establish a new practice for ThoughtWorks globally, focused on amplifying the impact of social effort by leveraging technology. In addition to leading this practice and providing strategic direction, Rohit also consulted for clients and worked as part of solutioning teams on complex implementations. Rohit holds a Master’s Degree in Engineering from IIT Kanpur. He is an avid reader and as part of his interest in social development, focuses on understanding societal structure, the inherent inequality therein and works with groups in an attempt to bridge the inequality.

Akash Agarwal

          Akash has over 15 years of experience leading software teams, focusing on various aspects of operations, strategy and engineering. Prior to co-founding Sahaj, Akash was responsible for leading an 800+ person strong organisation of ThoughtWorks India in the capacity of Managing Director and COO. As part of his tenure there, Akash was hugely successful in setting strategy, streamlining operations and improving profitability manifold, all the while adopting an open, collaborative style of leadership. Before joining ThoughtWorks, Akash played various technical and leadership roles at HCL, Infosys and Aditi Technologies. Akash holds a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering from Nagpur University. He is deeply passionate about sustainability on earth, specifically around sources of renewable energy.

Sunder Malyandi

          Sunder has more than 19 years of experience leading engineering teams, focusing primarily on solutions, delivery practices, risk management and operations. Prior to co-founding Sahaj, Sunder served as Vice President at ThoughtWorks. In this role he was responsible for Delivery & Engineering for ThoughtWorks India, where he was successful in transforming the Delivery organisation to be more consultative and outcome focused, in addition to significantly increasing the share of business from India-based customers. Before joining ThoughtWorks, Sunder played various technical & leadership roles at Infosys. Sunder holds a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering from Osmania University, Hyderabad. His areas of interest include unstructured data and distributed computing. He also shares a passion for wild life conservation and spends his free time reading, traveling and photographing wild life.

Nitin Dhall

          Nitin has over 12 years of experience spread across product management, consulting and establishing & leading IT organizations and delivery teams. Prior to co-founding Sahaj, Nitin served as the General Manager of ThoughtWorks Gurgaon, where he was responsible for establishing the culture of the organization and taking on complete P&L responsibilities. He has led a number of agile teams in delivering complex distributed enterprise solutions, including being the lead for one of the largest agile projects in the world. Nitin has also served a stint at TCS as part of the BFSI products group. Nitin holds a Master's Diploma in Business Management from Goa Institute of Management, in addition to a Bachelor’s Degree in Materials Engineering. His other passions include education, environment and rural economies. He is part of the Corporate Advisory Board of Chandigarh University and is an avid supporter of Greenpeace and RangDe.

Balachander Swaminathan

          Bala has over 17 years of industry experience spread across Agile and Lean Software Development, Process Consulting, Delivery Organization Setup and Operations, Product and Program Management, Talent Incubation, Training and Leadership Development. He has done extensive work in the lean and agile software development space, has published research papers in leading publications as part of his Doctoral research and is a popular speaker at conferences. Prior to co-founding Sahaj, Bala held key management positions in India and overseas at ThoughtWorks, MphasiS and Aptech, after doing his formal education from VJTI Bombay and IIT Bombay. Bala is a strong proponent of living in harmony with nature and can often be spotted in circles with associated activities in sustainability, organic farming, natural learning and localisation.

Rohit Bansal

          Rohit has more than 20 years of experience in leading engineering teams with a focus on guiding organisational behaviour. Prior to co-founding Sahaj, Rohit played multiple leadership roles with ThoughtWorks for more than 15 years. Rohit established the operations of ThoughtWorks India in 2001 and was one of the pioneers in evolving the practices for distributed agile development. His interest in social development led him to establish a new practice for ThoughtWorks globally, focused on amplifying the impact of social effort by leveraging technology. In addition to leading this practice and providing strategic direction, Rohit also consulted for clients and worked as part of solutioning teams on complex implementations.
